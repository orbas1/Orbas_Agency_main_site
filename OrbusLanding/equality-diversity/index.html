Equity at Blackridge Group Ltd

Equality & Diversity Commitment

A promise to foster fair opportunities and representation across every team, product and partnership inside the Orbas ecosystem.

Our Principles

Blackridge Group Ltd believes that diverse perspectives and equitable access create better outcomes for our customers, partners and the people who power Orbas AI, Orbas Freelance, Orbas Learn and Orbas Services.

We are deliberate about recognising bias, removing structural barriers and championing inclusion in the digital economy we serve.

Inclusive People Practices

  • Structured hiring, promotions and remuneration reviews with calibration against market benchmarks.
  • Inclusive onboarding and mentorship pathways tailored to the needs of distributed teams.
  • Employee resource groups championing communities including women in tech, LGBTQ+, neurodiversity and caregivers.

Product & Platform Inclusion

  • Accessibility standards built into design reviews, including WCAG 2.2 alignment across all platforms.
  • Bias testing and human oversight for AI models and algorithms deployed via Orbas AI and Orbas Learn.
  • Fair work policies that protect freelancers, educators and consultants engaged through Orbas Freelance and Orbas Services.

Supply Chain Responsibility

  • Partner selection criteria that consider diversity metrics and ethical compliance.
  • Integration with our Modern Slavery Statement and ESG roadmap.
  • Annual reviews with key suppliers to track progress on inclusive practices.

Measuring Progress

Representation Goals

We have set multi-year goals to increase representation of women, ethnic minorities and underrepresented groups across leadership, product and technical roles.

We publish these targets internally and review progress quarterly through our People & Impact Council.

Inclusive Experience Score

Employee surveys and platform user research provide qualitative and quantitative feedback on belonging, fairness and accessibility.

Results are integrated into the metrics reported in our Impact centre.

Accountability in Action

  • Policy Framework: Equality & diversity principles are embedded in our employee handbook, supplier contracts and product governance checklists.
  • Training & Awareness: Mandatory inclusion training for all employees, with specialised programmes for managers, product teams and algorithm developers.
  • Speak-Up Culture: Confidential reporting channels managed by the Ethics Office, with clear escalation pathways and non-retaliation commitments.
  • Community Investment: Partnerships with education providers and non-profits to expand access to digital skills training through Orbas Learn.
