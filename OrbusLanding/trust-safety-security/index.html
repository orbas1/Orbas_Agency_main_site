Security & Resilience

Trust, Safety and Security Statement

Protecting the organisations, experts and learners who rely on the Orbas ecosystem is a core responsibility of Blackridge Group Ltd.

Security Governance

Our trust and safety framework covers Orbas AI, Orbas Freelance, Orbas Learn and Orbas Services. It aligns with ISO 27001 practices, UK GDPR requirements and industry regulations relevant to our clients.

A cross-functional Security Council chaired by the Chief Information Security Officer reports to the Blackridge Group Ltd board on a quarterly basis.

Platform Security

  • Encryption in transit and at rest across our infrastructure and data stores.
  • Secure software development lifecycle with automated scanning and peer reviews.
  • Continuous monitoring, threat detection and 24/7 incident response capability.

User Safety

  • Robust onboarding and verification for clients, freelancers and partners.
  • Clear codes of conduct, dispute resolution processes and support services.
  • Content moderation and AI governance to prevent misuse or harmful outputs.

Business Continuity

  • Resilient multi-region infrastructure with disaster recovery testing.
  • Comprehensive playbooks for cyber incidents, data breaches and service outages.
  • Regular tabletop exercises with executive leadership and key suppliers.

Privacy & Data Protection

We operate a privacy-by-design approach across all products. Data handling is governed by our Privacy Policy and reinforced through technical and organisational measures.

  • Role-based access control and multi-factor authentication for employees and partners.
  • Data minimisation, retention schedules and secure disposal processes.
  • Third-party risk management covering cloud, data, payment and talent suppliers.

Our Safety Programmes

  • Trust Desk: A central team that responds to user concerns, fraud reports and policy violations.
  • Secure Development: Mandatory security training for engineers and threat modelling for new features.
  • Compliance Assurance: Independent assessments and penetration tests conducted at least annually.

Report an Issue

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability or safety concern, please contact us immediately.

  • Security Operations: security@orbas.io
  • Safety & Conduct: safety@orbas.io
  • Ethics Hotline: ethics@orbas.io

Continuous Improvement

We continually invest in automation, analytics and training to strengthen trust across our ecosystem.

  • Quarterly reviews of policies aligned to our ESG & CSR commitments.
  • Collaboration with industry bodies and regulators to anticipate emerging risks.
  • Public transparency via the Impact centre and Release Notes.
