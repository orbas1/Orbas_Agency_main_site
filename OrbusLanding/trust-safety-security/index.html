Security & Resilience

Trust, Safety and Security Statement

We are a growing start-up and we take pragmatic steps today to protect the people using Orbas while building stronger controls as the ecosystem scales.

Security Governance

Our trust and safety approach covers Orbas AI, Orbas Freelance, Orbas Learn and Orbas Services. As a lean team we follow UK GDPR and recognised security good practices, documenting each control so it can mature with the business.

Founders and technical leads review risks monthly, track incident response readiness and report progress to the Blackridge Group Ltd board.

Platform Security

  • Encryption in transit, routine patching and access reviews for our core infrastructure.
  • Source control, peer review and dependency scanning on every release.
  • On-call founders monitor key services with escalation to external specialists when required.

User Safety

  • Manual onboarding checks for clients, freelancers and partners before access is granted.
  • Clear community standards, a simple dispute process and human review of reported issues.
  • Early-stage AI governance that keeps a human in the loop for sensitive prompts or outputs.

Business Continuity

  • Daily backups stored off-site and periodic restore tests.
  • Lightweight runbooks covering cyber incidents, outages and customer communications.
  • Quarterly resilience reviews with our key cloud and service vendors.

Privacy & Data Protection

We currently hold limited personal data and we are preparing to introduce usage analytics within the next six months. Every new feature is assessed against our Privacy Policy, with documentation that makes future audits straightforward.

  • Role-based access, multi-factor authentication and logging for people who handle customer information.
  • Data minimisation, short retention windows and secure deletion when information is no longer needed.
  • Contracts with cloud, data, payment and talent partners that include confidentiality and breach reporting duties.

Our Safety Programmes

  • Trust Desk: A small cross-functional team triages user concerns during UK business hours and follows up within two working days.
  • Secure Development: Engineers complete lightweight security briefings and document threat considerations for each release.
  • Compliance Assurance: Annual independent testing is planned once platforms reach general availability; interim reviews are performed internally.

Continuous Improvement

Our roadmap focuses on building measurable controls without slowing product delivery.

  • Rolling policy reviews every six months, aligned to our ESG & CSR commitments.
  • Implementing security and safety metrics as we begin structured user analytics over the next half year.
  • Sharing notable updates in our Impact centre and Release Notes.
